Danger!

Dippy’s on a secret mission and must not be seen! Unfortunately, there are dragon-phoenix guards all over this place.

A guard is represented by a number in a shape. Each guard is aware of activity in the space that they’re in—Dippy definitely doesn’t want to visit a space occupied by a guard.

In addition, the two types of guards can detect activity around them in a different way.

  • Arrow guards can see in the direction of their arrow. The guard’s number denotes how far in that direction they can see.

  • Square guards can see all cells in a square around them. The guard’s number denotes how far in each direction the square extends.

Help Dippy get to the exit to reveal what they are doing by embarking on this mission. And remember—there’s danger at every turn!
