Solution: Oodles of Doodles
Answer: I'M NOT A RAPPER

Written by EpicToast™

Then, solvers can treat the circle grid as a “clock” and connect the numbers that correspond to the rhyming pairs (e.g., CHAIR is sent at 11 and SQUARE is sent at 4, so the dot at 11:00 is connected to the dot at 4:00). Doing so gives “USE THE OTHER ONES” from the letters not on the lines and “ANSWER I'M NOT A RAPPER” from the letters on the lines.
